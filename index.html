AlieNighTown is the game created by everyone in the Multimedia/Media Arts Game Design course (MMEDIA 3L03) at McMaster University (Winter 2021). Dance through 4 levels while collecting 7 dance moves! Created with Godot game engine and Blender.
Credits: 3D Art: Kenneth David, Ben Lee, Nicholas Verspaget, Nadeen Abdalaal, Nate Legros, Shuhang Dai, Taylor Jupp; Level Design: Fizza Ahmed, Tsz Chun Derek Chan, Andrew Veinot, Marco Ricciuti; Composition: Connor Anelli, Aidan Denommee; Sound Design: Duncan Simmons, Dalton Mark, Merilyn Wang; Programming: Jason Luu, David Ogborn; UI: Megan Barnard
